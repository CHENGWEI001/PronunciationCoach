Understanding the Map

A side-view (sagittal) cross-section guide

T

The Purple Shape (Tongue)

Your most active articulator. Watch it rise to touch the roof (Palate), pull back to the Velum, or protrude for Dental sounds.

W

White Rectangles (Teeth)

Fixed reference points. Helpful for anchoring the tongue tip or measuring jaw gap.

L

Red Curves (Lips)

Vital for Bilabial (P, B, M) and Labiodental (F, V) sounds. They animate to show closure or rounding.

A

Dashed Blue Line (Airflow)

Visualizes breath movement. A solid blockage means a 'Stop' sound; continuous flow through a gap means a 'Fricative'.
